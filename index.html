What is Autoship?

Autoship

Want to pay less for your pet food every time?

Your pet’s favourite products, at your door when you need them. It’s super easy to set up and you can adjust your order at any time. Available on all the essentials – dog food, cat food, pet flea & tick treatments, treats and cat litter. What’s not to love? Set your Buddy’s favourite products on autoship today.

  • You set the schedule, cancel at any time.
  • Add or remove items easily
  • It's all automated, you never need to worry about running out again

Browse

Simply shop online and choose your Buddy’s favourite autoship item(s).

Schedule

On the product page or at the shopping cart, choose how often you’d like your item(s) delivered. Whether it’s every week or every 6 months, choose the frequency that suits you, then go through the checkout to confirm your order.

Relax

Your delivery is on it’s way!
